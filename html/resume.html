American Singer/Songwriter

As a singer, songwriter, rapper, and actress, Kesha Rose Sebert has sold over 33 million combined tracks and over 55 million worldwide to date. Listed 26th on the Billboard's decade end charts from 2010-2019, she has received multiple awards and nominations. As a songwriter, she has also co-written songs such as "Till the World Ends" for Britney Spears and songs for Miley Cyrus, Ariana Grande, and Miranda Cosgrove.
